DREAMQII

Over the summer of 2015 I worked at DreamQii Inc. DreamQii a start-up creating a modular drone, able to be upgraded and easily stored in a backpack for travel. I worked with all components of the drone to design, build, test and prototype aspects in a hardware-in-the-loop lab.

A large part of my role at DreamQii was designing and performing the required tests to determine optimal hardware components for the DreamQii Plexidrone. Using a custom built hardware test lab I implemented an Arduino based program to give full sensor data output and allow for testing of individual components.

I also worked to develop a testing and developer platform to allow PID control tuning of the PlexiDrone. The goal of this was to provide a testing platform capable of auto-tuning each drone prototype.
