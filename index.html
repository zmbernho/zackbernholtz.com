ABOUT ME

My name is Zack Bernholtz, I am an engineer who loves to bring hardware products from design to market. I've worked in five different companies in many consumer electronics, industrial electronics, and automobiles working through the entire product life cycle. I deeply understand the complexities that go into bringing ideas into the market. To effectively implement a product you need a large knowledge base from user experience, engineering, manufacturing, cost assessment, assembly, testing, and lifetime overview. I have worked to be able to effectivley analyze a product from each of these key aspects. I am a graduate of the Systems Design Engineering program at the University of Waterloo. My primary focus in my studies and work experience has been on product development.

On a more personal note I am a great lover of the outdoors, cooking delicious meals, and travel.
