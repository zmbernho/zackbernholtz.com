BENDIX COMMERCIAL VEHICLE SYSTEMS LLC

Bendix is located in Elyria, Ohio and makes braking systems for medium to heavy-duty trucks. I was an intern focused upon the HIL (Hardware-In-The-Loop) lab, running automated tests on all aspects of our braking systems.

A large part of my role at Bendix was using real data to improve the ABS (Antilock Braking System) testing and automating a number of testing procedures. I also worked to install and debug sensors and hardware that was added to the lab to ensure accurate real-time sensing of all pneumatic components.

By automating the testing procedures a full test for a new vehicle design, or changing the hardware in a vehicle was able to be done over the course of a weekend, rather than by an operator over the course of a week, this saved a large amount of time and money for the testing team at Bendix.

Another project I worked on while at Bendix involved the lane assist technology. Bendix lane assist works in the case of a driver falling asleep or drifiting lanes while driving. The assist technology would detect the lane and vibrate the driver's seat to alert them to the drift direction to allow them to make corrective action. Lane assist also required a front-facing camera to allow for video review of lane assist events. I was involved in adding in the necessary hardware, reviewing output data and improving accuracy for lane assist technology.
